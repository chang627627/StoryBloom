The day becomes a story.

Before bed, the child makes four nonverbal choices. A color for the day, the weather inside, the shape of the feeling, a little friend for company. No typing, no reading required. Designed so a three year old can answer before they have the words.

Goodnight, moon garden

In the moon garden, all the flowers were going to sleep. A gentle owl said goodnight to each one. It had been a starry kind of day, and its heart still felt very still. Then the cozy sky pulled a soft blanket of moonlight over everything.
